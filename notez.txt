The name Abhinav Gupta refers to several distinct individuals, most notably the 10th-century Indian philosopher and modern researchers in technology and medicine. 

Abhinavagupta (c. 950–1016 CE) was a towering polymath from Kashmir, recognized as the pinnacle of Kashmir Shaivism.  He was a philosopher, mystic, aesthetician, and logician who wrote over 35 works, most famously the encyclopedic treatise Tantrāloka and the aesthetic commentary Abhinavabhāratī on the Nāṭyaśāstra.
